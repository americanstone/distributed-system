News

Fall 2021: 6.S081 will be taught in person (32-123, Mon. and Wed. at 1PM). The first class will be on 09/08/21. 6.S081 is AUS subject intended for undergraduates, and it provides an introduction to operating systems. Separately, 6.828 will be offered in future terms as a graduate-level seminar-style class focused on research in operating systems.

Questions or comments regarding S6.081? Send e-mail to the course staff at 6S081@lists.csail.mit.edu.
